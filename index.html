about Me

Let me introduce myself

I am a froth-year student from the School of Interactive Art. My goal is to design and build products that are human-centered and add value to life. I hope my project can contribute a unique lens for designing things. I have experience in web design, model design, game design, and tangible interaction. I hope to apply for an interaction designer in the future. I am passionate about design especially for the model/material design.

My personal inspiration to design thinking, collaboration, and design assessment is to ensure a practice that does not miss the mind and the heart of the product users. The conceptualization of the products starts by thinking about the beauty of humans in regard to affordances and signifiers; the perceived benefits of a product. On the other hand, it tasks me as a designer to involve the users by involving them to establish their inherent needs and preferences.
